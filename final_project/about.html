Bakery Story

Fresh Bakes began as a small idea between friends who shared a deep passion for baking and a belief that good food brings people together. What started as a home kitchen experiment quickly grew into a neighborhood favorite, built on dedication, creativity, and a love for quality ingredients. From the very beginning, our goal has been simple: to create baked goods that feel both comforting and exciting at the same time.

We focus on blending traditional recipes with modern flavors, allowing us to offer something for everyone. Whether it is a classic buttery croissant or a unique seasonal dessert, every item is made in small batches to ensure freshness and attention to detail. We work closely with local suppliers to source high-quality ingredients, and we are committed to being transparent about what goes into our products.

At Fresh Bakes, we also understand that customers have different dietary needs. That is why we offer a variety of options, including gluten-free, vegan, and nut-aware selections. We believe everyone should be able to enjoy delicious baked goods without compromise.

Over time, Fresh Bakes has become more than just a bakery—it is a place where people come to relax, connect, and enjoy something special. Whether you are stopping by for a quick breakfast, ordering a custom cake, or exploring our weekly specials, we are always happy to serve you with care and passion.

Lessons Learned

Background Image

I think a background image makes a web page more beautiful, but it is more confusing when it's time to use it. You need to choose an appropriate image, and after you choose it, make sure it is readable with the text color. I've tried changing the text color many times, but it turned out none of the colors worked well with the picture. So I had to choose another picture, but after I found one, I realized that I did not want it to repeat because the image would look awkward. However, if I didn't repeat the image, it would leave a big empty space, which I didn't want. So I thought I would find two similar pictures, one horizontal and one vertical, so that they would both look good on mobile and PC. However, the vertical pictures I found were good quality until I rotated them for PC screens. So finally, I decided to find an image that would be repeatable and still look good.
